CompArative GEne Cluster Analysis Toolbox

    Discover the essentials of gene cluster analysis with CAGECAT, your scientific tool for in-depth exploration of gene clusters. CAGECAT simplifies comparative analysis by integrating advanced NCBI searches for accurate homology searches and visualisation modules generating publication-quality gene cluster comparison figures. It is designed for researchers seeking a straightforward yet powerful approach to uncovering evolutionary relationships and taxonomic distributions in genomic research.
